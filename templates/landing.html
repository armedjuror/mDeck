Why this exists

It started with one specific problem.

My mother teaches higher-secondary mathematics with a textbook and a board. She kept asking me to make teaching decks for her chapters. PowerPoint took about 40 minutes per chapter. AI tools produced slides as images — fine at first glance, but impossible to fix when a formula was wrong, because changing one symbol meant regenerating the whole slide.

The fix was simple: give an AI a chapter PDF and mDeck's connection, and get back a markdown deck. Every formula is a text string. Every fix is a text edit. You and the AI keep working on the same file — nothing is locked inside an image.
