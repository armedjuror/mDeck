## Relations and Functions
### Class 11 · NCERT Chapter 2

Given two sets $A$ and $B$:
$$A \times B = \{(a,b) : a \in A,\ b \in B\}$$

---

## Key Properties

- If $n(A) = p$ and $n(B) = q$, then $n(A \times B) = pq$
- $A \times B \neq B \times A$ in general
- $A \times \phi = \phi$
